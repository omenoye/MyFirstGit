Company Background


  Lifestyle Travels & Tours Ltd was incorporated in March 2013. Its corporate headquarters is currently located in Lagos, Nigeria. We are one of the leading specialist destination travel company, passionate and focused on creating a trouble free travelling . Lifestyle Travels will handle your entire specific needs enquiries and requirements. A winning combination of dedication and experience with professionalism and the personal touch. Lifestyle Travels is in Partnership with New Castle TRAVELS AND TOURS LIMITED incorporated to carry on business as Travel Agents, Tourist Agents and contractors. The Company, since incorporation and commencement of business in January 1997, has grown rapidly over the years, delivering exquisite services and innovative programmes, tailored to suit Customers’ needs. The Company obtained the International Air Transport Association (IATA) License in June 1998; and it is a Member of National Association of Nigeria Travel Agents, (NANTA)
